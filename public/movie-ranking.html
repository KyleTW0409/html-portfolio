The best movies according to Kyle

Top 3 movies...

Number 1: Spider-man: Across the Spider-Verse

Went into the theatre not really having any expectations and came out legitimately dumbfounded by how good it was.

Number 2: Avengers Infinity War

An epochal film, one that I can legitimately watch over and over again with sheer enjoyment.

Number 3: Perks of being a wallflower

A touching film with endearing characters that makes you want to live life to the fullest.
